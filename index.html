About Audiocoustics

Audiocoustics provides a highly personable service backed by decades of experience. We've been at the forefront of audio and video technology throughout both the analogue and digital eras.

There is no substitute for experience. We can inject new life into your home or vehicle by integrating modern wireless and digital technologies with existing systems. Our expertise spans everything from simple upgrades to complex multi-zone installations.

We specialize in installs, removes, refits, and upgrades for cars, boats, trucks, and caravans. Our services extend to residential and commercial properties, delivering professional installations at homes, businesses, and major shopping centres across Cape Town.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

Do you really come to my location?

Yes! That's our specialty. We're a fully mobile service - we come to your home, office, or any location across Cape Town. No need to travel or drop off your vehicle.

How long does installation typically take?

It depends on the service. Car audio installations typically take 2-4 hours, CCTV systems 4-8 hours depending on cameras, and gate motors 2-3 hours. We'll give you an accurate time estimate with your quote.

What areas do you service?

We service the greater Cape Town area including City Bowl, Northern Suburbs, Southern Suburbs, Atlantic Seaboard, and West Coast areas. See our Service Areas section for specific suburbs.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes! All our installations come with a workmanship warranty. Products carry manufacturer warranties which vary by brand. We'll provide full warranty details with your installation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, EFT/bank transfer, and card payments. Payment is due upon completion of installation. Deposit may be required for large installations.

Can I supply my own products?

Yes, we can install products you've purchased. However, we can only warranty the installation, not the products themselves. We also source quality products at competitive prices.

Do you offer emergency services?

For security-related issues (alarms, CCTV failures), we offer priority service. Contact us via WhatsApp or phone and we'll assist as quickly as possible.

How do I get a quote?

Easy! WhatsApp us at 081 598 7961 (fastest), call us, or complete our online enquiry form. If needed, we can visit your location for an assessment before providing a detailed quote.
